Privacy Policy

Welcome to FZ Fassara. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by FZ Fassara and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account, we securely collect your username, email address, and a hashed version of your password. We also automatically track your watch history and video downloads locally to improve your personalized streaming user experience inside the application interface.

2. Google AdMob Advertising

This application integrates third-party tools like Google AdMob to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements. These service providers may use specific user tracking identifiers and cookies to serve ads based on your generic user interests.

3. Data Security Control

We implement professional cryptographic measures (scrypt password hashing) to ensure that your digital personal account details remain completely protected from unauthorized data breaches or database system vulnerability exploits.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or administrative inquiries regarding our privacy standards configuration enforcement, please reach out directly via our official system control panel.
